Pihu

“Some people feel like calm. She feels like meaning.”
I love the way her mind works.
She lives in books,
thinks deeply,
and feels quietly.
She carries a kind of calm that doesn’t ask for attention.
It simply exists — and changes the space around her.
She knows what she likes.
Simple things.
Honest choices.
Quiet comfort.
She romanticizes life.
Slow walks. Soft thoughts.
Somewhere in her mind, Paris always exists.
She finds peace in little worlds.
In focus. In stillness.
In moments that feel like rest.
I don’t love her loudly.
I love her intentionally.
In quiet ways.
Every day.
